My Creative Project for the OpenSesame Software Engineering Internship

1. How do you see OpenSesame fitting into your career journey or long-term goals?

OpenSesame coincides with my vision for personal growth as a Software Engineer. My desire is to be in a company where education, artificial intelligence, and practical engineering coexist. Your commitment to upskilling, constant development, and genuine development methods such as XP is precisely what I have been seeking.

My ultimate aim is to create tools that will make learning not only more accessible but also more individualistic. OpenSesame is already creating such products on a huge scale, and being part of that would aid my transformation into a Software Engineer who is able to create software, which would make the transition to higher positions in one’s career and life easier.

2. What strengths, skills, or perspectives will you bring to OpenSesame to make an impact and contribute to our mission?

I bring a combination of curiosity, solution finding capability, and the projects I have done in Python, Java, SQL, C++, HTML and even COBOL. My passion is to dissect complex issues, pose inquiries, gain knowledge at a quick rate, and fearlessly explore new tools.

I have constructed systems for data tracking, reporting, automation, and object oriented design. I am very skilled in collaboration, communicating ideas, and being organized, which are abilities that are important in XP, pair programming, and fast paced engineering teams.

However, what is most important is that I have an attitude that is in sync with your culture: the one of never ending learning, never ending improvement, and always trying to know the “why” of everything you create.

3. What excites you most about working with AI, and how do you think it can shape the future of learning and work?

Among all AI capabilities, the removal of barriers is the one that interests me the most. AI can take a vast world of information and make it personalized with the right explanation, the right example, and the right moment.

In education, this would mean a complete transformation. Training would no longer be one size fits all but rather, AI training would be personalized according to one’s pace, job role, skill gaps, and even their goals. Besides, when AI is integrated into tools correctly, it does not replace but rather empowers people.

OpenSesame is already moving in that direction with AI-powered curation, content creation, and smarter recommendations. I want to be part of the team that keeps pushing those ideas forward and helps shape the future of how people learn at work.
